About Us

Welcome to Crif Spices, a family-owned and operated restaurant serving delicious food for over 20 years. Our restaurant is located in the heart of the city and offers a cozy and comfortable atmosphere that will make you feel right at home.

Our Menu features a variety of dishes made from fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. From classic burgers and fries to exotic seafood, we have something for everyone. We also have a full bar with a wide selection of beers, wines, and cocktails.
